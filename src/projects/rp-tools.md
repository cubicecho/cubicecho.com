rp-tools is a client-side app for running a tabletop RPG character: track spell
slots, browse and prepare spells, flip to a play view during a session, and
keep a character sheet with computed save DC and attack bonus plus short- and
long-rest handling. Everything runs in the browser.

Stack: React + Vite + TypeScript, Tailwind, Radix UI.
