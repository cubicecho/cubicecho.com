ravocal helps a group land on a decision together: propose the options —
meeting times, places, whatever's up for a vote — collect everyone's ranked
preferences, and let ranked-choice voting settle on a winner that the most
people can live with.

Stack: TypeScript. Early-stage; source is on GitHub.
